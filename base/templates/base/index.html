About Us

The main objective of this project is to develop a web application for Cab booking solution for students. We are solving the problem of waiting cabs around universities for students by starting the service near every university and providing them a web-based application where they can book a cab online and get the ride booked within minutes. Students can save some money by opting for the subscription plan based on monthly/semester or yearly and the facility to ride together (Carpool). We also provide car rental services where students can get a car at pointed location and gives an edge to leave the car at the nearest destination cab center
